Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENT

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons,juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks,beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Make Lemon Filling:In a madiun saucepan
    • Whisk together 1 cuup sugar,flour,cornstarchh,and salt.
    • Stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg tolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while strring constantly until thhick.
